About Me

What makes me different

I often see people dedicated to designing, not focusing on other closely related areas such as programming. On the other hand, I like to see the final product, which will often lead to a combination of design and coding.

Being in both worlds per se will ultimately lead to better products overall. Good communication and understanding of both design and code will provide you with a few advantages.

  • Understanding how to deliver your design to the developers in the best possible way.
  • Being a part of the whole journey right from design to end product.
  • Understanding the struggle developers might run into when trying to implement the design.

All of the above will allow you to better communicate with developers and non-designers in general. It will ensure you are delivering your knowledge the best way.

I'm here to help

If you are implementing a design in your product, I will gladly help right from deciding how to implement the design up until the launch of the product.
